Bootloader Unlocking

  1. Enable Developer Options.
  2. Connect device to PC.
  3. Hold Volume Up + Volume Down.
  4. Enter Bootloader Unlock Mode.
  5. Press Volume Up to unlock.

ROM Installation

  1. Ensure latest stock firmware.
  2. OEM Unlock enabled.
  3. Download OFOX/TWRP and VBmeta.
  4. Enter Download Mode & Flash recovery through Odin.
  5. Disable Auto Reboot.
  6. Boot into recovery & Wipe partitions.
  7. Enable Sideload Mode & Sideload ROM.
  8. Format Data.
  9. Reboot & Enjoy.
